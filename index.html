About Us

Locally Brewed Coffee

Our coffee is sourced locally within the East Midlands, mostly within Nottingham. We also support independant businesses within our community.

Video Games for all!

Our Video Game Library includes games from a wide variety of genres! Whether you want to play some childhood classics or the latest releases, we cover all generations and platforms.

Board Games

We also have a huge selection of Board Games for all our customers to select from. We have all the current top-sellers as well as some classic family games, solo games and co-operative games.

Daily and Weekly Events

We host Daily and Weekly Events including Video Game tournaments, Trading Card Game Pre-Release Events and How To Plays for new and popular board games.

Free-Play Table Bookings

Do you just want a space to play your own board/trading card games? We offer Table Bookings for groups up to 8 people so you can all hang out and play together.

Friendly Staff!

Our staff are always around to help you with anything you need! We're also happy to offer advice on what games you should buy and offer you some company if you're a solo player.
